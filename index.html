Surya Narayanan - Quizmaster & Co-Founder

A passionate quizzer since the age of 10, Surya Narayanan has been hosting quiz shows since the age of 16. Known for his stage presence, the unstoppable puns, and keeping the audience and the participants at the edge of their seats, Surya has hosted several live quiz shows for clients including the Embassy of the Netherlands, LTIMindtree, Amul India, Protein and Nutrition Foundation of India, to name a few. He has also hosted quiz shows on several Television channels and is a co-host of India's only competitive quiz for special children. He is one of India's popular quizmasters to have hosted quizzes in 20+ destinations including Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kochi, Mumbai, Delhi, and Kakinada.

Mukilan A - Quizmaster & Co-Founder

A passionate quizzer turned quizmaster, Mukilan is an alumnus of the prestigious Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad (IIMA). Known for crafting questions that make the audience go "wow", Mukilan is a known face among the college and open quizzing circuits, and has hosted quizzes for several institutions including PSBB Schools, PSG Group of Colleges, IIM Ahmedabad, and Rotary International. He has also hosted several quizzes and competitions for students and societies across the world.

Samyuktha R - Quiz Host & Content Creator

An avid quizzer turned quizmaster, Samyuktha brings expertise in providing gamified knowledge solutions and engagements for schools, colleges, and corporate institutions. Having over five years of experience in the engagement space she has hosted quizzes across cities such as Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Ahmedabad, among others. One of the few professional woman quiz masters in the country, she was the external consultant and quiz master of Anvesha - a Nationwide quiz hosted by the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation - Govt of India. Her fun yet workout-able style of framing questions and engaging the audience make her on-ground and virtual events highly interactive. .
